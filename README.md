## Hi my names Jullian
Hi, I'm a web developer with a background in customer service and team leadership. I have a passion for creating beautiful and eye catching designs and a desire to tinker and  figure out the inner workings of systems. This led me to study in the world of tech. Being able to make things that people can use is one of the things I love about being a dev and drives me to learn new things every day and work on random personal projects.

I’ve loved tech since I first got my hands on a PC at a young age. Over time I’ve built several PCs from scratch and developed a habit of pulling things apart to understand how they work and how they can be improved. I enjoy building in both hardware and software, including designing and printing my own projects with a 3D printer. Last year, I created a web tool to help my dad manage his consulting work, tracking time, resourcing and processes. While building this, I hit challenges with React that pushed me to join Dev Academy and fully commit to becoming a developer. Now that I have, I wish I had done it sooner.
